Insurance Claims Analysis Dashboard(interactive Dashboard creation using PowerBI)
Project Objective
To develop an interactive Insurance Claims Analysis Dashboard that monitors insurance business performance by analyzing premiums, claims, policy activity, risk levels, and agent performance. The goal is to generate actionable insights that support better business decisions, improve claim processing efficiency, and identify areas for risk reduction and customer retention.
Business Questions Solved
What is the total premium collected across all policies?
How many claims were raised and what is their approval status?
What is the overall loss ratio of the business?
Which policy type generates the highest premium?
Which region contributes the highest premium and claims amount?
How many active and lapsed policies exist?
What is the average settlement time for claims?
Which agents perform best based on premium collection?
Which policies are classified as high-risk?
What is the claim approval percentage?
How does premium performance change over time?
What factors impact policy retention?
